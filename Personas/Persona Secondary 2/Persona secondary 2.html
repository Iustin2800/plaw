Detailed Description

Alex Popa is a teacher at a computer science faculty in Iasi, Romania. His dreams and passions are teaching, he wanted to be the best teacher someone could ever had. So he pushed hard through his studies and helped everyone that asked for help along the way, in order to become what he is today.

Alex is married and has no children. Outside of job, he likes reading in his free time in order to feed his hunger for knowledge and to keep himself up-to-date with the technologies in his field. He is a positive and happy person, which loves helping others with everything he can.

Around faculty Alex is considered one of the "cool" teachers as he is able to bound with every student and inspire them to learn more. His goal for his classes is to boost problem solving skills along with innovative thinking and social skills. Lately, he observed that his old fashioned technique is not as good as used to be with the older generations of students and he wants to update it.
